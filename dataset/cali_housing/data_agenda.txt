longitude: A measure of how far west a house is; a higher value is farther west,
latitude: A measure of how far north a house is; a higher value is farther north,
housing_median_age: Median age of a house within a block; a lower number is a newer building (Integer),
total_rooms: Total number of rooms within a block (Integer),
total_bedrooms: Total number of bedrooms within a block  (Integer),
population: Total number of people residing within a block (Integer),
households: Total number of households, a group of people residing within a home unit, for a block (Integer),
median_income: Median income for households within a block of houses (measured in tens of thousands of US Dollars),
ocean_proximity: Location of the house w.r.t ocean/sea (Categorical),
BinaryHouseVal: a binary value to indicate if the house price is higher than 1.5 multiplication
