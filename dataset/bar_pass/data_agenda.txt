decile1b: Binary variable indicating if a graduate is in the first decile,
decile3: Variable related to the third decile of graduates,
decile1: Variable related to the first decile of graduates,
sex: Gender of the graduate (categorical: Male/Female),
lsat: Law School Admission Test (LSAT) score,
ugpa: Undergraduate grade point average (GPA),
grad: Year of graduation,
fulltime: Indicates if the graduate pursued full-time or part-time studies,
fam_inc: Family income of the graduate,
gender: Duplicate attribute representing gender,
race1: Race or ethnicity of the graduate,
tier: Tier or ranking of the law school attended,
bar1: Binary variable indicating if the graduate passed the bar exam on the first attempt
